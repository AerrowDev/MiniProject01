GENERAL

No. Even though we like our furry friends, we are no longer able to allow pets within the hotel. After a few months in business, we've had owners place their adorable pets on the beds and tops of dining tables. In several cases, dog owners even brought their canines into the pool. Pet feces was frequently ignored.
We made the decision to forbid the bringing in of pets in order to avoid any problems, such as pet allergies, sanitary conditions, etc.
Please leave your dogs at home, even though we recognize that this limitation will disappoint some individuals.
Pets can be any animal, including but not restricted to dogs, cats, birds, and reptiles.

No, we do not serve food at the resort. However, you are more than welcome to bring your own food and drinks (no corkage fee). You can also cook at the resort; we have a gas stove that you can use. We also have a BBQ grill and refrigerator that you can use free of charge.
There are a few restaurants like Max's Restaurant, near the resort (about a 5 to 20 minute drive). We suggest using Google Maps or Waze to find nearby restaurants.
For your convenience, here's a link to get you started: Google Maps (Nearby restaurants).
If you will be needing catering services or food delivery,we have a few contacts that we can refer . Contact details will be provided as soon as your reservation has been confirmed. Kindly coordinate directly with our catering partners for rates and planning.

We only have the basics: medium-sized pot & pan, knife, and chopping board. Please feel free to bring your own as needed.
Please be informed that you are not allowed to bring your own stove (gas & electric).

We only allow 1 car to be parked inside the resort. We have a parking lot across the street, exclusive for guests of Al Fresco Springs. Vehicles can also be safely parked along the curb just outside the resort; there should be more than enough space.
We are located in a guarded subdivision, where guards patrol throughout the day and night, so rest assured your vehicles will be safe.
There is no charge for parking.

Please remember to bring your own towels, toiletries, and tableware, as these are not provided by the resort. Blankets are available upon request from the caretaker for no extra charge. Pillows and bed sheets are provided.
